BellGen

Produces patterns based on the included bell SynthDef.

Basic Usage:

(where intensities is an array of numbers from 0 to 1) 

BellGen.new.genPattern(intensities)

The genPattern Algorithm:

  • Choose a base frequency multiplier.
  • Choose whether all notes should be the same, or whether they should vary randomly.
  • Choose whether the rhythm should be subdivided into 2, 3, or 4 sections.
  • If randomly chosen to do so (based on intensity), further subdivide each section into two sections. Each of those subsections may then again be further subdivided into two sections once more.
  • Randomly (weighted by intensity) choose which sections and subsections will be played.

Class Methods:

  • *new Stores the bell SynthDef.

Instance Methods

  • chooseActive(active,subdivisions,depth) Determines which of the subdivisions will be played. The number that will be played is active. depth is the depth of the subdivision (0 for top-level sections, 2 for twice-nested subsections).
  • subGen(intensity,subdivisions,depth) Generates subsections as necessary with a maximum depth of 2.
  • genPattern(intensities,base = 2,voice = 0) Generates and returns a pattern based on intensities. base is the base length of the pattern. voice is used as a key to the pattern for debugging purposes.
